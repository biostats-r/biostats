1 Introduction

Data collection is the systematic gathering of data for a specific reason and the data is usually used for downstream processing in statistical analysis.

In this book, we will discuss a reproducible data management workflow. First, we will show you how to design spreadsheets for data collection. Second, we will discuss the whole life cycle of data, from data collection to sharing data. And finally, we have one chapter on how to best curate your data.
